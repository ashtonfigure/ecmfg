About our exclusive company
E Coast Manufacturing (M) Sdn Bhd, established in 2015, has evolved from its origins as a trading company, which was founded in 2008. Headquartered in Lahat, Ipoh, Perak, Malaysia, we specialize in the precision manufacturing of high-quality fishing singkers. With a strong commitment to innovation and excellence, we also provide comprehensive export solutions to meet the demands of markets.
